BMR Calculator

Your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body burns at rest to maintain vital functions like breathing and digestion.

Why is BMR Important?

Knowing your BMR helps you create an effective diet and fitness plan. It allows you to:

  • Estimate your daily calorie needs
  • Set weight loss or muscle gain goals
  • Improve your overall health and metabolism
